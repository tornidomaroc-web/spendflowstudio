Common questions

Which apps do the templates work in?

Every template works in both Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets, online or offline. You choose whichever you prefer, and you can switch anytime.

Do I need to be good with spreadsheets?

No. You replace the sample numbers with your own, and the file does the math, the charts, and the formatting for you. Nothing to build.

How do I get my files after buying?

Your download is available right after checkout. You get an access file to open in Google Sheets or download for Excel, so you can start the same day.

Can I use a template on my phone?

Yes. The files work on a computer, tablet, or phone. For Google Sheets you only need a free Google account.

Is my financial data private?

Completely. Your numbers stay inside your own file and are never sent anywhere. Nothing syncs, and there is no account to create.

Do I pay again for updates?

No. When a template gets better, the update is free. You buy a file once and keep it for good.

What is your refund policy?

Because these are instant digital downloads, they are generally non-refundable once delivered. If something is wrong or a file will not open, email us and we will make it right.

Do you take custom requests?

Sometimes. If you have an idea for a tracker you wish existed, send a note. We read every suggestion and it may become the next template.
