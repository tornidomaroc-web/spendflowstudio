Our story

We build calm tools for life's biggest numbers.

SpendFlow started from a simple frustration. Money tools are either too basic to trust with a real decision, or so complex they need a manual and a weekend to set up. We wanted something in between: spreadsheets that feel calm, look considered, and actually do the math for you.

What we believe

Three things we hold to

Money should feel calm, not loud.

Most finance apps shout at you with alerts, streaks, and guilt. We think a good money tool should feel like a quiet desk and a clear head. You open it, you see where you stand, you close it, and you get on with your life.

A tool should do the work.

You should type a number once and watch the sheet handle the rest. Every SpendFlow file runs on real formulas, so the charts, totals, and scores update themselves. Nothing to build, nothing to maintain.

You should own what you buy.

No subscriptions, no logins, no data sent anywhere. You buy a file, you download it, and it is yours to keep and use for good, in Excel or Google Sheets, on any device.

Our craft

How we make them

Every template is built and tested by hand, one at a time. We care about the small things: a number format that reads cleanly, a color that means something, a layout that fills the page instead of leaving you guessing. If a sheet would not help us with our own money, it does not go in the shop.
